Kevin Durant

The Slim Reaper

Here is a brief timeline of Kevin Durant’s life:

  • September 29, 1988 : Kevin Wayne Durant was born in Suitland, Maryland
  • Childhood : Durant grew up loving sports with his siblings. His grandmother, Barbara, was a strong influence, telling him that his height was a blessing.
  • College : Durant played for the University of Texas Longhorns in Austin. As a Longhorn, Durant started every game during his freshman year of college, averaging 25.8 points per game and 11 rebounds per game in 35 games.
  • 2007 : Durant was chosen second overall in the 2007 NBA draft by the Seattle SuperSonics.
  • 2007-2016 : Durant played for the Seattle SuperSonics, which became the Oklahoma City Thunder after his Rookie of the Year campaign.
  • 2014 : Durant was voted the league’s Most Valuable Player.
  • 2016-2019 : Durant led the Golden State Warriors to back-to-back NBA championships.
  • 2019-2023 : Durant signed with the Brooklyn Nets in the summer of 2019.
  • 2023 - Present : Durant is currently playing for the Phoenix Suns.
      • Throughout his career, Durant has won numerous awards and honors, including two NBA championships, an NBA Most Valuable Player Award, two Finals MVP Awards, four NBA scoring titles, and has been named to ten All-NBA teams
